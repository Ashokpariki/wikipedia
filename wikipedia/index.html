Pariki Ashok

Pariki Ashok (born on 1 june 2001) is a student in Vaagdevi Engineering College from Warangal in Telangana.

He is pursuing final year in B-tech in Vaagdevi Engineering College.He completed his Intermediate in Kakatiya Graduates Junior College in Narsampet with 95.8%.He completed his SSC in Z P P Secondary School at Upparapally with 85%.

Coming to his family,his father and mother named as Sambaiah and Anasurya both are farmers.He have a elder brother named as Nagaraju. He got married with Anusha in 2020 and they blessed with a son named as Dinesh.
